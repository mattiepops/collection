Matei's Film Collection

Here are some of my all time favourite films. They are great suggestions for if you ever find yourself mindlessly scrolling on Netflix, to find something to watch, and you actually spend more time scrolling than enjoying a film!

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty
Ben Stiller
Adventure Drama

A man living a dull life dreams up romantic and action-filled scenarios in order to escape from monotony. When his own job is threatened, he sets out on a real romantic and action-packed journey.

Go somewhere
Mr Nobody
Jaco Van Dormael
Sci-Fi Drama

In 2092 the last mortal human on Earth reflects on his long past and thinks about the possible lives he might have led, based on once choice he did or did not make as a child.

Spirited Away
Hayao Miyazaki
Adventure Anime Fantasy

During her family's move to the suburbs, a sullen 10-year-old girl wanders into a world ruled by gods, witches, and spirits, and where humans are changed into beasts.

Good Will Hunting
